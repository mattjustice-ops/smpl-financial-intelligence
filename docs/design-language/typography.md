# Typography

## Hierarchy

| Element | Size (pt) | Weight | Color |
|---------|-----------|--------|-------|
| Slide title | 26 | Bold | Navy |
| Subtitle | 12 | Regular | Gray |
| Section label | 11 | Bold | Green |
| KPI value | 22 | Bold | Navy / tone |
| KPI label | 10 | Regular | Gray |
| Body / bullets | 11 | Regular | Black |
| Table cells | 10 | Regular | Black |
| Commentary header | 13 | Bold | Orange |
| Footnote | 9 | Italic | Gray |
| Chart title | 10 | Bold | Navy |
| Chart axis | 8 | Regular | Gray |

## Rules

- **One headline per slide** — no stacked H1/H2 in visual zone
- **Key takeaway** — single line under KPIs when `story_slide`; not duplicated in footer
- **Numbers** — `$k` / `$M` consistent with `fmt_money`; parentheses for negatives in tables
- **Truncate** — titles > 56 chars for chart archetypes (enforced in chart primitives)
